Being a 2024 WOW (Week of Welcome) Leader

Project Information

  • Project Name: Being a 2024 WOW (Week of Welcome) Leader
  • Date: Fall Quarter 2024
  • Overview: I was the leader of a group of 35 incoming transfer students. I planned an entire week and kept the trains running for a great WOW experience.
  • Features: Leadership of 35 transfer students for a full week. Organized and executed a detailed schedule of events and activities. Ensured the safety and engagement of all group members.
  • Outcome: My WOW group loved the week we spent together, and I ended up staying friends with most, if not all, of my "Wowies."

Description

Cal Poly, like many universities, has a Week of Welcome (WOW) where incoming students, both freshmen and transfers, are assigned a group to spend the week exploring San Luis Obispo with. As a WOW leader, I was in charge of everyone's safety and ensuring everyone was having a good time. This was a great leadership experience as managing 35 people required strong organizational and communication skills. I also learned how to foster a sense of community and belonging among my group, which was incredibly rewarding.
